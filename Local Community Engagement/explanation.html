Explanations

To use generational aid you agree to the following:

CiviLink is not meant to be a social media page. It's strictly meant as a help and volunteer page

No personal information will be stored. This app is only meant to help connect people

The main goal of Generational aid is to connect older people with younger people. Elders often times are lonely and like to talk with youth. What’s in it for the youth is being able to learn new skills from the elders and leveling up in the local leaderboard!
